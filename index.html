What is this graph about?

This graph shows the hourly average Karma Point of the Reddit posts split between Jpg/Gif images from the hot tab of the r/dankmemes subreddit.

How is this calculated?

It first looks at the 7 days most recent dankmemes hot posts. Then it uses cumulative moving average to calculate the mean of hourly posts from that time period.

What can this be used for?

Give insights into the trend of posting either animated or still memes into r/dankmemes. Other than that, not much at its current stage honestly. However, this will serve as a foundation for me to build and add more feature to this website
